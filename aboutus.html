Our Story

Founded in the heart of Phnom Penh, Phka Malis Restaurant has been serving authentic Cambodian cuisine for over two decades. Our journey began with a simple vision: to preserve and celebrate the rich culinary heritage of Cambodia while creating a warm, welcoming space for families and friends to gather.

Every dish we serve tells a story—a story of tradition passed down through generations, of fresh ingredients sourced from local markets, and of the passion and dedication of our talented team. From our signature Amok to our fragrant Kuy Teav, each recipe is crafted with love and respect for our cultural roots.

Today, Phka Malis stands as more than just a restaurant. We are a community hub, a place where memories are made, and where the flavors of Cambodia come alive. Join us on this delicious journey and experience the warmth of Khmer hospitality.

The Atmosphere

Step into Phka Malis and be transported to a world where traditional Cambodian elegance meets modern comfort. Our restaurant features warm wooden accents, soft ambient lighting, and carefully curated decor that pays homage to Cambodia's rich cultural heritage.

Whether you're enjoying an intimate dinner for two or celebrating with family and friends, our spacious dining areas offer the perfect setting for every occasion. The gentle sound of traditional Khmer music in the background creates a soothing ambiance that enhances your dining experience.

Our outdoor terrace provides a serene escape, where you can dine under the stars while surrounded by lush tropical plants. Every corner of Phka Malis has been thoughtfully designed to make you feel at home while experiencing the authentic essence of Cambodian hospitality.
